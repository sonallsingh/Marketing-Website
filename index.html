Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Comfort Bite

How does the subscription work?

Our subscription is simple! You choose a weekly or monthly plan, tell us your meal preferences (veg/non-veg, lunch/dinner), and we deliver hot, fresh meals to you every day. You can pause, cancel, or change your plan anytime.

Can I customize my meals?

Yes! For our monthly subscribers, we offer customization. You can let us know your dietary restrictions, spice preferences, or any specific allergies, and your assigned 'mom chef' will prepare the meals accordingly.

What about hygiene and food safety?

Hygiene is our top priority. All our kitchens are 100% certified, follow strict FSSAI protocols, and are subject to regular audits. Our 'mom chefs' are trained in safe food handling and use only fresh, high-quality ingredients.

What areas do you deliver to?

Currently, we deliver across all major localities in Bangalore. We are expanding to Mumbai and Pune soon! Enter your pincode on the 'Get Started' page to check if we deliver to your location.

Can I try before committing to a monthly plan?

Absolutely! You can opt for our 'Veg Weekly' plan to try our service for 7 meals. We're confident that one bite will have you convinced.

What if I'm not satisfied with the food?

We have a "love it" guarantee. If you're not satisfied with a meal for any reason, please let us know through our 24/7 support. We will provide a full refund for that meal, no questions asked. Your feedback helps us get better!
